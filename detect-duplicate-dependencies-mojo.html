duplicate-dependencies:detect-duplicate-dependencies

Full name:

com.github.marschall:duplicate-dependencies-maven-plugin:1.1.1:detect-duplicate-dependencies

Description:

Generates a PKCS12 truststore from a collection of certificates located in a folder. Runs during a normal Maven build.

Attributes:

  • Requires a Maven project to be executed.
  • Requires dependency resolution of artifacts in scope: runtime.
  • The goal is thread-safe and supports parallel builds.
  • Binds by default to the lifecycle phase: verify.
